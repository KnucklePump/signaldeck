How to read this report

MacroDriver Weekly translates live macro data into actionable market signals. It is built in layers — start at the top for the big picture, then drill down into the sections that are most relevant to your decisions. Each section is designed to stand on its own or be read in sequence.

1Current Economic Regime

Identifies the dominant macro regime driving markets right now and assigns probability weights to four possible scenarios. Sets the directional bias for everything that follows.

2Driver-Asset Impact Matrix

A heat-map showing every active driver's effect on every asset class at a glance. Use it to spot where forces are stacking up or cancelling out.

3Economic Driver Deep Dives

The evidence behind every signal. Live indicator readings, breach levels, and Short / Medium / Long forecasts with specific winners and losers — including your watchlist stocks.

4Economic Asset Class Forecast

A consolidated Outperform / Neutral / Underperform view across 15 asset classes over three time horizons. Your macro-driven positioning playbook.

5Economic Watchlist Forecast

The macro backdrop translated directly to your holdings — showing whether each stock faces a tailwind, headwind, or neutral macro environment across all three horizons.

6Net Capital Flow Forecast

Where money is being pushed by macro forces. Separates Real Money (institutional, slow-moving) from Fast Money (hedge funds, momentum) so you can see who is likely to move markets.

7Economic Forecast Calendar

The next two weeks of scheduled macro events with the MacroDriver forecast vs market consensus — and what moves in your portfolio if the forecast is correct.

8Driver Interactions

Shows where drivers overlap and how double-counting is prevented — so you can trust that the signals in this report reflect genuine independent forces, not noise.

9State Snapshot

Continuity data for the next weekly run — tracks driver changes and forecast accuracy over time.
