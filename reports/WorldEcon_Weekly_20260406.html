§4 G20 Macro Cycle Map

🌱 EXPANSION
GDP rising, unemployment falling, confidence high. Policy tightening begins. Credit easy, corporates investing.
🔺 PEAK
Growth decelerating, inflation elevated, policy tightening. Sentiment turning. Leading indicators rolling over. Cycle near its top.
📉 CONTRACTION
GDP falling or stalling, unemployment rising, confidence dropping. Easing begins. Credit tightening. Recession risk elevated.
🌀 TROUGH
Growth bottoming, unemployment peaking, aggressive easing underway. Sentiment at lows. Early stabilisation. Setup for next expansion.
🔄 RECOVERY
Growth turning positive from a trough. Unemployment stabilising. Policy still accommodative. Early cycle — risk assets typically outperform.
🔥 OVERHEATING
Growth above potential, inflation well above target, structural distortions building. Unsustainable — correction typically follows.

Momentum scores: −1.0 (rapid deterioration) → 0 (stable) → +1.0 (rapid improvement). Reflects rate of change within a phase, not the phase itself.
