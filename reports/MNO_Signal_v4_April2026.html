Pillar 1: Business Quality — 65/100

Company Overview

Meridian Mining is a UK-domiciled, TSX-listed copper-gold developer focused on the Cabaçal VMS (Volcanogenic Massive Sulphide) belt in Mato Grosso, Brazil. The flagship Cabaçal Au-Cu-Ag project is a past-producing mine (operated 1987-1998) being advanced toward restart. A Pre-Feasibility Study (March 2025) delivered exceptional economics, and the Definitive Feasibility Study is expected in Q4 2026.

Reverse DCF / Implied Probability Analysis

For a pre-revenue developer, the "reverse DCF" question becomes: "What probability of successful mine construction is the market pricing in?"

At C$1.94 (EV ~US$620M): The market is pricing in roughly 63% probability that the Cabaçal PFS base-case NPV of US$984M will be realized. Given that the project has a Preliminary Licence, 89% Proven reserves, a 17-month payback, and a DFS on track for Q4 2026, a 63% probability seems conservative. The spot-case NPV of US$1.41B implies even greater upside if commodity prices hold.

Interpretation: The market is pricing in meaningful execution/permitting/financing risk, which is appropriate at PFS stage but looks increasingly conservative as DFS delivery and permitting milestones are met. Score: 68
