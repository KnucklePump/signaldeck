G20 Cycle Map

🌱 Expansion
GDP rising, unemployment falling, confidence high. Policy begins to tighten. Corporates investing, credit easy.
🔺 Peak
Growth decelerating, inflation elevated, policy tightening. Sentiment turns. Leading indicators rolling over. Cycle near its top.
📉 Contraction
GDP falling or stalling, unemployment rising, confidence dropping. Central banks begin easing. Credit tightening. Recession risk elevated.
🌀 Trough
Growth bottoming, unemployment peaking, aggressive easing underway. Sentiment at lows. Early stabilisation signals. Setup for next expansion.
🔄 Recovery
Growth turning positive again from a trough. Unemployment stabilising. Policy still accommodative. Early cycle — risk assets typically outperform.
🔥 Overheating
Growth running above potential, inflation well above target, structural distortions building. Unsustainable — correction typically follows.

Momentum scores range from −1.0 (rapid deterioration) to +1.0 (rapid improvement) and reflect the rate of change within a phase, not just the phase itself.
